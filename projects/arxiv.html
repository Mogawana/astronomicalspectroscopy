Astronomical Spectroscopy

Using Spectroscopy to discover our Universe. A NASSP Honours Course

ArXiv Project

Due: 21 April 2016

Type: Individual

Description: The goal of this project is to summarize a recent paper on astro-ph that is related to spectroscopy. The student should select a paper from Recent astro-ph list that is related to spectroscopy and provide a maximum of a 1000 word summary of the paper and the underlying spectroscopic principles of the paper. The student can include figures in the summary. The paper choice should be email to the lecturer by 13 April.

Submission: Pdf versions of the reports can be submitted via email to the lecturer with 'Arxiv Project' in the subject line and the file attached named with "arxiv_surname.pdf".

Grading: The project will be worth 10 points. Grading will be based on the quality of the presentation and summary of the work.
